Summary

Three friends, George, Harris, and J, and their dog Montmorency decide to go on a boating trip up the Thames River to improve their health.

It starts with the friends planning the trip and preparing for it. They pack only the things they need, and set off up the river in a rented boat.

As they row up the Thames, they has to face a few challenges. However, they continue to enjoy their trip. The book is filled with many funny moments.

In addition to their challenges, the three friends also encounter strange incidents along the way, such as being attacked by aggressive swans and discovering a woman's body floating in the river. However, they continue on their journey.

When the weather turns bad, three friends are forced to leave their boat and spend the last day of their trip at a pub. They enjoy a delicious meal and reflect on their journey up the river.
